Hip Hop
Hip hop is a genre of popular music developed in the United States by inner-city African Americans and Latino Americans in the Bronx borough of New York City in the 1970s.
Pop
Pop music is a genre of popular music that originated in its modern form during the mid-1950s in the United States and the United Kingdom.
Indie
Indie rock, a subgenre of rock music, originated in the UK, US, and New Zealand in the early to mid-1980s, but by the 1990s, it became associated with the music produced by such bands.
Jazz
Jazz is a music genre that originated in the African-American communities of New Orleans, United States, in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
Rock
Rock music is a broad genre of popular music that originated as rock and roll in the United States in the late 1940s and early 1950s.
Country
Country music, also known as country and western, originated in the southern United States in the early 1920s.
Electronic
Generally referred as EDM, this form of music is produced by DJs who add dozens of tones to a piece to create unique music.
Classic
Classical music is art music produced or rooted in the traditions of Western culture, including both liturgical and secular music.
